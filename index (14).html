Do You Still Have Questions?

Schedule a consultation with us to get all of your answers; otherwise, here are some of the most asked questions.

What is College Funding Finders?

A college funding resource is your guide through the college costs process. We offer personalized tools for building a college list, understanding financial aid, comparing costs, and every other step along the way.

How do I choose the right college planning resource?

Finding the right resource involves research. Look at the data sources and methodology, but also look at user testimonials. Compatibility is also key—you should feel confident the tools understand your goals and needs.

Is College Funding Finders free to use?

Yes, you can explore our college search tools and access general information at no cost. Some advanced features and personalized consultations may require registration, but basic exploration is completely free.

What's the difference between a high school counselor and these tools?

High school counselors typically advise hundreds of students a year and tend to offer broad advice. Our tools, on the other hand, work with your specific situation and consider your family's unique financial circumstances.

Are college funding tools worth it?

Consider the average cost of a four-year college education—over $100,000 for many schools. Expert guidance during this crucial time will help you maximize financial aid, unlock scholarship opportunities, and make decisions that save you money down the line.

When should I begin planning for college costs?

Junior year is when many families get serious about college planning, but starting as early as freshman year allows you to make strategic decisions about academics and finances. We firmly believe it's never too early to start planning for your future.
